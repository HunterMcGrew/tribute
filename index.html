About Skrillex

Skrillex is an American DJ and musician. Before taking on the stage name "Skrillex" he simply went by "Sonny". Skrillex's real name is Sonny Moore.

Before becoming a solo artist Sonny was in a post-hardcore band "From First to Last" as the lead singer from 2004-2007. During this time he was in the band they recorded two studio albums before leaving to pursue a solo career.

In 2009, he released the Gypsyhook EP. He was also suppose to record his debut studio ablum named "Bells". Instead, he stopped production of the album and began performing under the name Skrillex. Releasing a free EP "My Name Is Skrillex".

Skrillex's career was really kicked off by the "Scary Monsters and Nice Sprites" EP in late 2010 and the "More Monsters and Sprites" EP mid 2011. In 2011, out of five nomination at the 54th Grammy Awards, he took home three. Over his career so far Skrillex has won eight Grammy awards. Making him the dance music artist with the most awards at this time.
